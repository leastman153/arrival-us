Welcome to Arrival US!

Arrival U.S. is here to serve you and your community. With a center open in Tijuana, we aim to service those in need of better opportunities, by fighting for them through the process of immigrating to the United States. We provide a multitude of services to our beneficiaries, including legal representation, documentation assistance, job searching workshops, and more. If you need help starting a new life in America, Arrival U.S. is prepared to make it happen.

Our Services

All of our services are free to everyone. We provide a multitude of services to our beneficiaries to make their immigration process as easy as possible and make thriving in America an effortless feat.

Our Goal

By 2020, we hope to have helped 1,500 immigrants make it to the U.S. efficiently, legally, and most importantly, safely, as well help another 2,000 immigrants obtain their U.S. citizenship by 2025. With our initiative, and your help, we can provide better lives for thousands of immigrants on their way to America.
