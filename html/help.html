Why

In some countries they aren’t always given fair opportunities. People don’t want to live in a place where they don't feel safe or can’t get the opportunities necessary for them to succeed. The problem is they cant get to the U.S to get the opportunities with how hard the whole immigration process is. That’s where Arrival U.S. comes in. We are starting with discussion boards for people seeking help to get help. A simple web forum where people seeking help can get live answers from the certified people to help. Such as, lawyers, people in law school, etc. As our company grows and the more support our company gets determines how much impact we can have, and if we can start centers for people to come to around the world where we would hire bilingual people to be at the center, to help people live at the centers, speeding up the process.
